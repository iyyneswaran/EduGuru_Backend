# EduGuru Backend

Welcome to EduGuru Backend, where we believe that learning should be both educational and entertaining! Here are some of the incredible features that make our project a real joyride:

---

🎢 **Feature 1: Course Management**  
Like a rollercoaster, our course management system takes you on an exhilarating ride through the world of education. Create, update, and delete courses with the speed of a rollercoaster, but without the nausea!

---

🧙‍♂️ **Feature 2: User Authentication**  
Our authentication system is like a wizard granting you access to a magical realm of knowledge! With just a flick (or click) of your wand (or mouse), you can sign up, log in, and start your adventure in learning!

---

📜 **Feature 3: Quiz Generation**  
Ever wanted to create quizzes that are as fun as eating cake? Well, now you can! With our quiz generator, whipping up multiple-choice questions is a piece of cake—without the calories!

---

💬 **Feature 4: Discussion Forums**  
Why have boring discussions when you can have lively debates? Our discussion forums are here to ensure that learning feels more like a friendly banter than a dull lecture. Bring your popcorn!

---

🎉 **Feature 5: Analytics Dashboard**  
Want to know how your learners are doing? Our analytics dashboard gives you insights that are clearer than a crystal ball. Track progress and engagement without needing a PhD in data science!

---

## Tech Stack 🛠️

- **TypeScript** (75.6%) - The backbone of our backend, keeping things typed and fabulous!
- **Python** (23.1%) - For those moments when you need to keep it Pythonic and simple!
- **Docker** (1.3%) - Container magic because sometimes you need to containerize your chaos!

---

## Getting Started 🚀

1. Clone the repository
2. Install dependencies: `npm install`
3. Set up your environment variables
4. Run the server: `npm start`
5. Start learning and having fun!
